## Limitations of this diff

- **Trait method counts** are folded into the trait, not separately diffed.
- **Macros** are not catalogued.
- **`pub(crate)` items** are deliberately excluded â€” they're internal.
- **Re-exports** (`pub use`) are not catalogued; only original
  declarations. This means a type that lives in `eo-core` and is
  re-exported from `orbit-geo` shows up once (in `eo-core`).
- **Generic parameters** are included in the signature hash via their
  names; renaming `T` â†’ `U` will hash differently. Future enhancement:
  normalise generic-param names too.
